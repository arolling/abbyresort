Year-round travel is possible to Hollandstoun Island, with creativity. In the summer our ferry from the Scottish mainland (6 hours) departs thrice weekly on a set schedule; fares can be booked in advance. Our winter ferry is provided by Mr Willingsworth (555-7734) on an as-needed basis; fares must be negotiated individually.

He has been known to accept whisky as payment.
